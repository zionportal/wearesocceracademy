Programs available for request

Age-based soccer options for Dr. Phillips families

Families can request information or registration help for We Are Soccer Academy age-group programs. Submitting interest does not claim a specific field, schedule, price, or open spot; it gives the academy the details needed to follow up.

  • Ages 3-5: first soccer experiences, movement, balance, listening, and ball comfort
  • Ages 6-8: dribbling, passing, shooting basics, teamwork, and simple game play
  • Ages 9-11: stronger technique, decision-making, spacing, and confidence
  • Ages 12-13: technical consistency, 1v1 habits, support angles, and tactical awareness
  • Ages 14-15: advanced training habits, speed of play, fitness, and leadership
  • Ages 16-18: mature decision-making, communication, pressure habits, and game understanding

Local parent FAQs

Which program should my child request?

Start with the age group that matches your player. If your child is new, shy, advanced, or returning after time away, include that context in the form.

Are exact fields, schedules, or prices listed here?

No. This page is for local guidance only. The academy can follow up after receiving your registration or information request.

Can beginners request a program?

Yes. Beginner guidance is part of the parent pathway, especially for younger players or families unsure where to start.

What if Dr. Phillips is not the right fit?

Use the nearby links on this page or submit the form with your preferred area so the academy can respond with practical guidance.
