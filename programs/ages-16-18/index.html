Ages 16-18 Soccer Program

High-intent training for older teen players who want stronger habits, better decisions, and confident play.

Register Your PlayerPrevious Age Group

Who it is for

For older players seeking purposeful development

This program is designed for older teens who can handle more focused training and want to improve technical reliability, game awareness, communication, and confidence.

Development goals

  • Improve speed and quality of decisions
  • Strengthen technical execution under pressure
  • Build leadership, communication, and resilience
  • Support mature training habits and accountability

Skills and concepts

Game-ready habits

  • Scanning before receiving
  • Playing quickly and safely when needed
  • Pressing, recovery, and defensive responsibility
  • Finishing choices and final-third composure

Session experience

Focused and competitive

Families should expect training that asks players to think, communicate, compete, and apply technique in realistic situations. No specific schedules, placements, or availability are promised on this page.

Confidence and teamwork

Players build confidence by learning how to contribute, communicate, handle pressure, and stay engaged as teammates.

Beginner guidance

What families should expect

Request help if unsure. If an older teen is new to soccer, families should share that context in the form so the academy can respond with guidance on an appropriate next step.

FAQ

Does this include college recruiting?

No recruiting claims are made. This page focuses only on player development.

Is this only for advanced players?

It is built for older teens, but families can request help if they are unsure about fit.

What should families expect?

Expect a focus on effort, habits, communication, technical quality, and game understanding.
