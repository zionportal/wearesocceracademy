Who it is for

For players ready for more purposeful training

This program supports players who need stronger fundamentals and simple tactical understanding. It can help beginners grow while giving developing players more structured repetitions.

Development goals

  • Improve first touch and ball control
  • Build passing, receiving, shooting, and 1v1 confidence
  • Introduce spacing, support, and transition moments
  • Develop effort, focus, and responsibility

Skills and concepts

Technique plus game awareness

  • Receiving across the body
  • Dribbling to beat or protect
  • Passing choices and movement after the pass
  • Basic attacking and defending roles

Session experience

Active reps and clear coaching

Families should expect technical activities, decision games, and small-sided play that keeps players involved. Coaching should help players understand what to try and why it matters.

Confidence and teamwork

Players build confidence by solving soccer problems, communicating with teammates, and learning that mistakes are part of development.

Beginner guidance

What families should expect

If a player is new or unsure, families can use Not Sure Yet on the registration form. The goal is to place the player where learning feels challenging but manageable.

FAQ

Is this only for experienced players?

No. It supports both newer and developing players with age-aware coaching.

What changes at this age?

Players begin connecting technique to decisions, spacing, and teamwork.

Will my child get individual feedback?

Coaching feedback is part of the learning environment, but no specific evaluation format is promised.
