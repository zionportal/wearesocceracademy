Ages 12-13 Soccer Program

Focused training for early teen players building sharper technique, better decisions, and stronger confidence.

Register Your PlayerNext Age Group

Who it is for

For players entering a more tactical stage

This program is for players who are ready to connect technical quality with decision-making, movement, and team responsibility.

Development goals

  • Sharpen first touch, passing, and receiving
  • Improve 1v1 attacking and defending habits
  • Introduce better spacing and support angles
  • Build consistency, focus, and communication

Skills and concepts

More speed, more awareness

  • Playing with head up
  • Changing point of attack in simple ways
  • Defending body shape and pressure
  • Transitioning after winning or losing the ball

Session experience

Training with purpose

Families should expect a blend of technical repetition, competitive activities, and game-like exercises. The goal is to help players understand how skills apply under pressure.

Confidence and teamwork

Players learn to communicate, compete respectfully, respond to coaching, and take ownership of effort and attitude.

Beginner guidance

What families should expect

Good fit depends on readiness. Beginners can still be supported, but families should share experience level in the form so the academy can guide the next step.
