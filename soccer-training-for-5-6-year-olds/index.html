Training focus

Better touches, better confidence

Players ages 5-6 are ready for more structured activities while still needing fun, movement, and encouragement. We focus on simple soccer skills players can understand and use.

  • Dribbling, stopping, turning, and ball control
  • Passing, shooting basics, and teamwork games
  • Listening, effort, and sportsmanship
  • Small-sided play that keeps kids involved
