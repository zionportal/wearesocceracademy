What players learn

Fun, simple, and age-appropriate

At age three, soccer should feel like a positive adventure. We use movement games, simple ball touches, coach-led activities, and encouragement to help players enjoy being active.

  • Balance, coordination, and body movement
  • First touches, dribbling games, and ball comfort
  • Listening, taking turns, and following simple directions
  • Confidence in a group setting
